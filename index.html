Frequently Asked Questions

Will this charge my MacBook Pro/laptop?

Absolutely! The NexCharger 800W delivers enough power to charge even the most power-hungry laptops like the MacBook Pro 16", Dell XPS, and gaming laptops. Each USB-C port can deliver up to 140W when needed.

How many devices can I actually charge at once?

You can charge up to 10 devices simultaneously - 8 via USB-C ports and 2 via USB-A ports. The smart power distribution ensures each device gets optimal charging speed based on its needs.

Is it safe to leave plugged in 24/7?

Yes! NexCharger features advanced safety protections including over-current, over-voltage, over-temperature, and short-circuit protection. It's designed for continuous use and will automatically stop charging when devices are full.

How fast does it charge compared to original chargers?

NexCharger charges as fast as or faster than your original chargers. For example, it can charge an iPhone 14 to 50% in just 30 minutes, and a MacBook Pro to 80% in under an hour.

What's included and what's the warranty?

You get the NexCharger 800W unit, power cable, user manual, and desktop stand. We include a 1-year manufacturer warranty plus 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied.

Is it really that much smaller than regular chargers?

Yes! GaN (Gallium Nitride) technology allows us to pack 800W of power into a device 50% smaller than traditional silicon-based chargers. It's about the size of a small book but replaces 5-10 separate chargers.
